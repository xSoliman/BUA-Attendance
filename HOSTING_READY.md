## 💰 Free Hosting Options

### Recommended: Render.com
- **Cost**: $0/month
- **Setup**: 10 minutes
- **Pros**: Easiest, automatic HTTPS, auto-deploy
- **Cons**: Cold starts (30s after 15 min idle)
- **Solution**: Use UptimeRobot (free) to keep warm

### Alternative: Railway.app
- **Cost**: Free first month, then ~$2-3/month
- **Setup**: 10 minutes
- **Pros**: No cold starts, better performance
- **Cons**: Limited free credit

### Best Performance: Vercel + Render
- **Cost**: $0/month
- **Setup**: 15 minutes
- **Pros**: Best frontend performance, no frontend cold starts
- **Cons**: Two platforms to manage
